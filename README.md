Last updated: March 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PlayPal Hub ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your
information when you use the PlayPal Hub mobile application.

PlayPal Hub provides a platform where players can organize matches, track results, and participate
in local sports communities.

**1. Information We Collect**

• Name or username
• Email address
• Profile photo (optional)
This information is used to create and manage your user account and profile within the app.
Activity and Match Data
• Match results
• Match history
• Player rankings
• Match invitations or challenges
• League participation
This information allows the app to maintain rankings, match records, and competition statistics.
Device Information
• Device type
• Operating system version
• App performance data

**2. How We Use Your Information**
• Create and manage user accounts

We do not sell your personal information.

rankings, and league participation.

operate the app.

**4. Data Storage and Security**
We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or
misuse. However, no system can guarantee complete security.

**5. Your Rights**

• Request deletion of your account and associated data
To request data deletion, please contact us using the email below.

**6. Children's Privacy**

personal information from children.
